About

I work with companies that have outgrown the spreadsheet, the side‑project, or the agency hand‑off — and need a partner who can move between architecture diagrams, Figma files, and a Monday morning roadmap conversation without losing the thread.

Before consulting, I co‑founded Gerber Labs, a platform that put custom printed circuit boards in the hands of students, hobbyists, and small businesses. That experience — building a product, a team, and a market at the same time — still shapes how I work: pragmatic, opinionated, and allergic to ceremony.
